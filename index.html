The Experience

When I heard that we were going on a field trip to ArcherMalmo, I was very excited mainly because I would be seeing dogs. When we arrived at the building the office setting was very open. I really liked that idea because it created a positive working environment to show the value of collaboration and team work. I also got to learn the day to day work habits of the programmers and designers. With that being said, my experience at ArcherMalmo was eye opening.

Favorite Parts

My favorite part about this field trip was getting to learn more about what goes on in a web development agency. I loved getting to walk around and see all the floors, that are all very different, but they all come together to make the customer happy and in the end a great final product. I really enjoyed seeing the Palm Beach website, as a teenage girl who tans there, I really appreciated the website you all designed! I give it two thumbs up! Okay, I also really liked seeing all the dogs running around when we first got there, I mean who wouldn't?

Career Path?

This is my first year in a web designing class. Though the coding part is not my favorite, I love the creative side.
